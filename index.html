What this CRM delivers

🎯

Lead Tracking

Capture leads from walk-ins, field events, online, and CSV imports with auto-generated lead numbers per branch.

💬

Interaction Timeline

Log every call, visit, and WhatsApp touchpoint with outcome notes and scheduled follow-ups.

📅

Follow-up Calendar

Monthly calendar view showing follow-up density per day. Overdue items surface in red in the task bubble.

🔄

One-Click Conversion

Convert a lead into a rosca-digital Subscriber directly from the lead detail page — pre-filled from lead data.

📊

Reports & CSV Export

Branch-wise and employee-wise conversion rates. CSV export matches the existing marketing tool format exactly.

🔔

Smart Notifications

CRM follow-ups appear in the existing task bubble. Daily 08:00 SMS digest for each employee's due leads.
